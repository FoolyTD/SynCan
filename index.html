SynCan

Using synthetic biology to sustainably synthesize cannabinoids.

Click for more information

Traditional cannabis cultivation yields the need to use large amounts of water, electricity, fertilizers, and pesticides. If the cannabis industry were to remain small-scale, perhaps the environmental burden would not be so great. However, with the dawn of legalization in many countries like Mexico and the USA, the cannabis market is poised to grow substantially. Therefore, there is a large opportunity to create a sustainable alternative to traditional cannabis cultivation. Using synthetic biology to engineer an organism that can produce cannabinoids, initially CBD, is the solution SynCan believes will lead to a sustainable cannabis market.
